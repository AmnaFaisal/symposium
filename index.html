International Postgraduate Cybersecurity, Artificial Intelligence, and Data Science Symposium 13th & 14th November 2024

Highlights

We are pleased to invite our esteemed academic partners to the International Postgraduate Cybersecurity, Artificial Intelligence, and Data Science Symposium at Taylor's University—a prestigious platform for postgraduate students to showcase their research to a diverse audience, including both academia and industry professionals. The 2024 symposium will be a hybrid event, held on the 13th and 14th of November at Taylor's University Lakeside Campus and online, offering flexibility for all participants.
Highlights of this year’s symposium include:

  • Keynote addresses from industry leaders and experts in the fields of cybersecurity, AI, and data science.
  • Presentations by our talented researchers, featuring both oral and poster sessions that highlight the latest innovations and findings.
  • Interactive forums designed to foster collaboration between academia and industry, providing valuable insights into the current trends and challenges.
  • Opportunities for networking and building partnerships across academic and industry sectors.
  • Engaging discussions on the future of technology and its impact on society.

We look forward to celebrating the innovative work of our postgraduate students with you and hope you can join us for this exciting event.

Registration

We are pleased to announce that registration for this year’s Postgraduate Research Symposium at Taylor's University is completely free for all participants, including those from academia, industry, and the corporate sector.
We warmly invite postgraduate students and faculty from universities around the world, as well as professionals from various industries and the corporate sector, to join us for this exciting event. Your participation will greatly enrich the discussions and collaborations that this symposium aims to foster.
